Instructions

The goal of Draw Something Game is to let the guess user correctly guess the given word from the drawing of the draw user.

Draw Uer Instruction:

  • Draw the given word in canvas area
  • You could choose color and strock thickness

    • Guess Uer Instruction:

  • According to the drawing, please guess the word
  • Put letters into the following blanks
  • Blank number is exactly the length of answer word
  • Click letter under the canvas area to put it into blank
  • Click the last filled in letter in blank to put letter back
  • If guess word is wrong, all blanks will be vacated
    • Word: {{game.get_answer()}}
