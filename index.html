Draw Something

Instructions

    The goal of Draw Something Game is to guess all words correctly from the users drawings. Two players will exchange roles as guess user and draw user each round. There are currently 30 words in the game.

Draw Uer Instruction:

  • Draw the given word in canvas area
  • Choose color and strock thickness by clicking
  • The first white color stroke could be used as eraser
  • Click the "Submit" button in the up right corner when you finish

    • Guess Uer Instruction:

  • Click "Play" button in up right corner to play the drawing
  • According to the drawing, please guess the word by putting letters into blanks
  • Blank number is exactly the length of answer word
  • Click letter below canvas area to put it into blank
  • Click the last filled in letter in blank to put letter back
  • If guessed word is wrong, all blanks will be vacated automatically
  • After correctly guessing a word, the page will automatically go to the draw page.
